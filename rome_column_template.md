# Jim Rome Column — Output Template

## Structure

### Owner Sections (one per owner, sorted by current rank, 1st → last)

**Header format:** `OWNER NAME — [pts] pts · [rank] · [one-line verdict/hot take]`

**Body:** 2-3 sentences of Rome-voice color commentary. Max 4 sentences.

**Bolding rule:** Bold any personal attacks, roasts, or direct shots at the manager. Ring names encouraged in bolded lines. Real first names in unbolded body text.

### Cross-Owner Narrative Section (optional)

When a storyline spans multiple owners (father-son saga, rivalries, draft karma), it gets its own named header (e.g. "THE FATHER-SON SAGA"). Not every column needs one — only when there's a genuine multi-owner narrative.

## Rules
- Jim Rome energy — confident, opinionated, sports-talk-radio
- No emoji, no icon fonts
- Skimmable — readers should find their owner in 2 seconds
- Ring names: Mustard Boy (Zach), Bubba G (Gunner), The Backpass Assassin (Gayden), Ghost Pepper (Devin), The Noisemaker (Rafe)
